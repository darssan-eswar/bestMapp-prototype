Problem Statement: The Best™ MApp

In the University of South Carolina, there is no one good way to figure out where to get to your classes as an incoming student. While a map of the buildings is provided, students are left clueless as to where in the massive buildings their classrooms are. Our app aims to provide an interactive and simple way to help not only students, but any UofSC affiliated members better locate their classes and efficiently navigate through campus.
